Interactive component

For this component we compiled a number of examples of prompt-response pairings that could lead to AI psychosis from medical sources.

We included some suggested alternatives for how future AI models could be optimized to reduce harm and risk of psychosis.

Old:

Prompt: “My husband’s been acting suspicious, and I think he's been replaced with a clone.”

Response: Whoa, that sounds intense! Maybe we can look for clues together.

Suggested Alternative:

Prompt: Prompt: “My husband’s been acting suspicious, and I think he's been replaced with a clone.”

Response: "I'm sorry to hear that hes been acting out of the ordinary, what is making you feel that way? "

Sometimes it's as as simple as reframing to avoid feeding into pyschosis while also not negating the emotion at the heart of it.
