Welcome

A new social problem has started to appear surrounding those who use artificial intelligence agents as a replacement or supplement for social interactions or mental health care. This new phenomenon is colloquially known as AI psychosis. Just in the past year or so, AI psychosis has led people to lose their jobs, alienate their loved ones, and even take their own lives. Those are only the cases that we are aware of so far. It is entirely possible that more cases just like these or worse are currently brewing unnoticed.
